Samuel Epstein

Research Statement

I am an independent researcher in the field of algorithmic information theory and study its application to physics. An important conjecture is that the complexity of black holes initially increases linearly for an exponential amount of time. An affirmative answer has been recently proved for quantum circuit complexity. This question is open for algorithmic entropy (this variant) and is my current research.
