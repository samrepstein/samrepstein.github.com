Research Statement

I am an independent researcher in the field of algorithmic information theory. My current manscript on algorithmic aspects of physics introduces several barriers between the quantum and classical realms. Given a POVM measurement, an overwhelming amount of pure or mixed states will produce algorithmic garbage, which are probability measures with negligible self-information. Also, with respect to a system-environment dynamics, an overwhelming majority of non-pointer states will decohere into algorithmic garbage.

This is because most quantum states have negligible algorithmic self information. However a wave function collapse can cause an uptake in algorithmic self information of the quantum state (imagine a PVM consisting of classical strings). This enables nonnegligible signals from further measurements and also enables partial information cloning. Due to conservation inequalities, quantum operations do not have this property.
